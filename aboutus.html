Our Team

Kwadjo Owusu Danso
Team Lead

Kwadwo, a Ghanaian, with over 5 years’ experience in game development, 3D modelling, game design as well as being proficient in C# and C++, has great passion for games and massive interest in art and creativity. His passion, drive, determination and experience are what he brings to each and every project worked on, to ensure its successful outcome.

Moses Adamu
Tech Lead

Moses is a Nigerian, who is a young, innovative and solution driven Information Technology Professional, who loves taking on new challenges, learning new processes and working with new people. He is mobile app developer, game developer, website developer, data scientist, Java, JavaScript and python programmer. He is a game enthusiast, with a great passion for the gaming industry and a desire to be one of the change drivers in the industry in Africa.

Esther Mwangi
Finance and Marketing Lead

Esther is a Kenyan with five years’ experience in sales & marketing plus finance. She is passionate about creating engaging and dynamic 3d games. She loves the thrill of building enterprises and working with teams of diverse experiences to create successful ventures.

Chevez Pakiry
Lead Artist

Chevez is from South Africa with over five years ‘experience in Graphic Design and 3D modelling & texturing. In addition to this, he also has over 2 years experience in the field of Self-Adhesive Label Printing. He is very passionate about the gaming industry and creating great designs.
